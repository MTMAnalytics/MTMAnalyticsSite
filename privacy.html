2. What Data We Display

MTM Analytics displays publicly available sports performance data, including:

  • Player names

This information is sourced from publicly available CueScore data, official websites, event photographers, and club social media pages.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process publicly available sports data under the GDPR legal basis of Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f)) for the purpose of sports reporting, analytics, and performance insights.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Browser and device type
  • Approximate geographic region
  • Traffic sources (e.g., search engines)

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

Because Google Analytics uses cookies, we display a cookie consent banner when you first visit the site. You may accept or reject non‑essential cookies at any time.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Account information
  • Passwords
  • Payment details
  • Private or sensitive personal data

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at matt@mtmanalytics.co.uk.
