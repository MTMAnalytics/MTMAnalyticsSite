Legal & Image Attribution

MTM Analytics displays publicly available sports performance data, including player names, match results, rankings, and profile images. This information is used to provide analytics and insights into cue sports performance.

Data Sources

Player names, match results, rankings, and some profile images are sourced from publicly available CueScore data and other public sources where permitted.

Image Sources

Player images displayed on MTM Analytics may come from the following sources:

  • CueScore player profiles
  • Official websites
  • Event and tour photographers
  • Publicly available club social media pages

These images are used under legitimate interest for the purpose of sports reporting and analytics. No private or sensitive personal data is processed by MTM Analytics in relation to these images.

Copyright & Attribution

Where applicable, images remain the property of their respective owners. MTM Analytics does not claim ownership of third‑party images and will provide attribution where known and practicable.

If you are a player, club, or photographer and would like an image credited or removed, please contact us and we will act promptly.
